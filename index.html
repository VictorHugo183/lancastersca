Welcome to Lancasters

Lancasters is a two partners firm of Chartered Accountants. Our organisational structure revolutionises the traditional accounting firm structure. We have a ‘real’ flat structure where all of our colleagues have responsibility for their respective clients. Therefore, you will have a direct communication line with the person responsible of your file. This frees up Partner time and enables the Partner in charge to work alongside the team member on high level issues and will always be available for review, advice or meet with you.
